# Roadmap

The evolution of the DIVE protocol will follow the standard Internet-Draft and IETF process.

## Direction

Future work will primarily consist of:

- Incorporating feedback from the IETF community
- Refining the protocol based on review and discussion
- Addressing interoperability, security, and deployment considerations raised during the draft lifecycle

## Process

Changes to the specification will be driven by:

- IETF discussions and mailing lists
- Feedback from reviewers and implementers
- Issues and proposals submitted in this repository

## Goal

The objective is to iteratively improve the DIVE specification until it reaches sufficient maturity for broader adoption and potential standardization.

---

No fixed feature roadmap is defined at this stage; development is feedback-driven and aligned with the IETF process.
